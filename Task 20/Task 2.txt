Virtual Monthly Low Budget Cost planning Assistant.

A model can  be created that analyses how you use money every month.
Given some data either real(from previous months) or test data it can find out what  is it that
is important to you and draw a month budget by carrying out the following duties:

1) Use the above data and do comparisons looking at shops you normally go to against online store
products data.
2) Compute time and distance taken to reach those stores
3) Look at social network marketplace that is closer to you (Data from facebook amongst the others)
4) Analyse the entire data and suggest three best option that will result in you using less money
5) Suggest alternatives based on given data
